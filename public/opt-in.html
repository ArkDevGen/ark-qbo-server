SMS Opt-In Process

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

How Clients Opt In

Ark Financial collects SMS consent from clients during the onboarding process. When a new client engages Ark Financial for accounting, tax, payroll, or bookkeeping services, they complete a client intake form that includes a dedicated SMS consent section.

The client provides their mobile phone number and signs the intake form acknowledging they agree to receive account-related text messages. No SMS messages are sent without prior written consent.

SMS Consent Disclosure

The following disclosure is included on the Ark Financial client intake form:

SMS Consent Disclosure

By providing my phone number below, I agree to receive account-related text messages from Ark Financial, including appointment reminders, payroll notifications, tax filing updates, and billing notices.

  • Message frequency varies (typically 1–5 messages per month)
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • Reply STOP at any time to opt out
  • Reply HELP for assistance

Consent is voluntary and is not a condition of service. For questions, contact Ark Financial at (402) 287-7085 or ark.devgen@gmail.com.

View our SMS Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.

Opt-In Methods

  • In-Person: Client signs the intake form during their onboarding meeting at our office
  • Engagement Letter: Client signs and returns the engagement letter which includes the SMS consent disclosure
  • Direct Request: Existing clients may contact our office to opt in to SMS notifications

Consent Record Keeping

All signed consent forms are stored in the client's file and maintained for the duration of the client relationship plus seven (7) years in accordance with our data retention policy. Consent records include the date of consent, the phone number provided, and the signed disclosure form.

Opt-Out Process

Clients may opt out of SMS messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message from Ark Financial
  • Calling us at (402) 287-7085
  • Emailing ark.devgen@gmail.com

Upon receiving an opt-out request, the client's number is immediately removed from all SMS communications and a confirmation message is sent.
