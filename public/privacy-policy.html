Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

1. Introduction

ARK Financial ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard personal information in connection with the accounting, tax preparation, payroll, and bookkeeping services we provide.

2. Information We Collect

In the course of providing our services, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Social Security numbers and tax identification numbers (for tax preparation services)
  • Financial records, bank statements, and payroll data
  • Business information for bookkeeping and accounting clients
  • Employment and wage information for payroll processing

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide accounting, tax preparation, payroll, and bookkeeping services
  • To communicate with you about your account via phone, email, and SMS
  • To send appointment reminders, filing deadlines, and account notifications
  • To process payroll and generate required tax documents
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. SMS Communications

ARK Financial may send SMS (text) messages to the phone number you provide for account-related communications. By providing your phone number and signing our client intake form, you consent to receive these messages from ARK Financial.

  • You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message from ARK Financial
  • For help, reply HELP or contact us at (402) 287-7085 or ark.devgen@gmail.com
  • Message frequency varies (typically 1–5 messages per month)
  • Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan
  • ARK Financial does not charge any fees for SMS messages

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information, including your mobile phone number, to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party providers who assist in delivering our services (e.g., payroll processors, tax filing systems, secure document storage)
  • Legal Requirements: Government agencies as required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Professional Advisors: As necessary to provide our professional services to you

All third-party service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are prohibited from using your information for any purpose other than providing the contracted services.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encrypted storage systems for sensitive financial and personal data
  • Secure client portals for document exchange
  • Restricted access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews and updates to our systems

7. Data Retention

We retain client records for a minimum of seven (7) years in accordance with IRS requirements and professional accounting standards. After the applicable retention period, records are securely destroyed using industry-standard methods.

8. Your Rights

As a client, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information held by ARK Financial
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP or contacting us directly
  • Receive a copy of your personal data in a commonly used format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable regulations. Any material changes will be communicated to clients directly via email or SMS notification. The effective date at the top of this document indicates when the policy was last updated.
